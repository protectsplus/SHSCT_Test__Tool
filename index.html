Section 4 — Overall risk rating *

RED or AMBER requires ligature rescue cutters and staff training.

■ RED — High risk
Care frequently delivered to patients with acute / severe mental illness. Most patients spend time unsupervised.
■ AMBER — Medium risk
Most patients spend long periods with minimum supervision. A number of unlocked accessible areas present.
■ GREEN — Low risk
Areas with good movement of staff and patients through well-observed, high-traffic spaces.

⚠ Ligature rescue cutters required. Staff training must be arranged.

RED: all core staff attend half-day Ligature Rescue training (2-yearly). AMBER: designated staff attend; others complete e-learning (3-yearly).
Training: safetyintervention.training@southerntrust.hscni.net
